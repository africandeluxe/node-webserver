Ceaser Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 head romaine lettuce, washed and chopped
  • 1/2 cup Caesar salad dressing
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt (optional)
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the chopped romaine lettuce, Caesar salad dressing, grated Parmesan cheese, croutons, black pepper, and salt (if using). Toss until the lettuce is evenly coated with the dressing and the other ingredients are well combined.
  2. Divide the salad among serving plates or bowls.
  3. Garnish the salads with additional grated Parmesan cheese and croutons, if desired.
  4. Squeeze fresh lemon juice over each salad just before serving for an extra burst of flavor.
  5. Serve the Caesar salads as a side dish or add grilled chicken, shrimp, or other protein for a complete meal.
